Cake

The aim of this project was to develop an iOS application which will allow a user to create an ‘event’ such as a birthday party and share it with other users, who can choose to accept, reject or select ‘maybe’ to the invitation. The event may also provide users with notifications based on their location. To achieve this, I have learned and coded in Swift.

In this system, a user can login with their email and password, or choose to register with a username, email and password. From the login page they are shown the home page containing a table of the events they are hosting. From this table they can choose to add a new event, edit a current event and invite users – there is a view for event editing/creation and a view for viewing an event/inviting users. An event consists of an event image, event name, location, start date and time, end date and time, description, and a list of people who are invited, who are going and who have chosen ‘maybe’. The description is limited to 500 words and the image is chosen from images I have provided.

Alternatively, there are two other tabs. On the homepage there may be a badge on the icon for the ‘attending events’ tab bar item, indicating that they have recently been invited to an event. When they click on this item, they are shown a table of events that they have been invited to, events that they are going to, and events that they have chosen to maybe attend. They can view the events and change their status – if they reject an event the event is removed from the table. When they click on the ‘account’ tab, they are shown their user page with their user icon and username. If they click ‘Edit’ on the navigation bar they can change their user image icon and username, and if they click ‘Account’ on the navigation bar they can change their email and password.

For this project I created my own icons and images in illustrator, I used Balsamiq Mockups 3 to create the wireframes and I used photoshop to create the screenshots.
